Delete Your Kharcha Account

Kharcha lets signed-in users permanently delete their account from the app or website.

How to request deletion

  1. Open Kharcha and sign in with the phone number used for your account.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. In Delete account, type DELETE.
  4. Select Delete My Account.

You can start from the sign-in page. After signing in, open Settings.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile, phone-based account record, and active sessions.
  • Grocery lists, pantry records, meal plans, chat history, and finance records owned by your account.
  • Your membership in shared grocery lists.

What may remain

If you contributed items to a grocery list owned by another user, those items may remain in that shared list without your account attached. Data may also be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, legal, or operational reasons.
